Cancellation Policy

Clearly Outlines The Terms And Conditions For Canceling Bookings, Including Applicable Fees And Timelines To Ensure Fairness For Both Users And Chauffeurs.

Last updated: January 1, 2024

Cancellation Fees

Time Before Pickup Cancellation Fee Refund Amount
More than 24 hours No fee 100% refund
12-24 hours 10% of booking total 90% refund
6-12 hours 25% of booking total 75% refund
2-6 hours 50% of booking total 50% refund
Less than 2 hours 100% of booking total No refund
No show 100% of booking total No refund

Examples

Example 1: Early Cancellation

Scenario: You booked a ride for Saturday at 3:00 PM. You cancel on Thursday at 5:00 PM.

Time before pickup: More than 24 hours

Fee: No cancellation fee

Result: Full refund processed

Example 2: Same-Day Cancellation

Scenario: Your ride is scheduled for 8:00 PM. You cancel at 3:00 PM the same day.

Time before pickup: 5 hours

Fee: 50% of booking total

Result: 50% refund processed

Example 3: Last-Minute Cancellation

Scenario: Your ride is at 10:00 AM. You cancel at 9:00 AM.

Time before pickup: 1 hour

Fee: 100% of booking total

Result: No refund

Frequently Asked Questions

You can cancel your booking through:

  • Your account dashboard on our website
  • Calling our customer service at +1 (234) 567-890
  • Using the cancellation link in your booking confirmation email

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. The time it takes to appear in your account depends on your payment method and financial institution.

Yes, you can modify your booking up to 12 hours before the scheduled pickup time without any fees. Modifications within 12 hours may incur charges based on availability.

If a chauffeur cancels your booking, you will receive a full refund immediately. We will also help you find an alternative chauffeur if needed.

We may waive cancellation fees in cases of:

  • Medical emergencies (with documentation)
  • Severe weather conditions affecting safety
  • Flight cancellations (with proof)
  • Other extraordinary circumstances at our discretion

Additional Terms

  • All cancellation times are calculated based on the scheduled pickup time
  • Corporate accounts may have different cancellation policies as per their agreements
  • Recurring bookings must be canceled individually
  • Promotional bookings may have special cancellation terms
  • This policy applies to all service types unless otherwise specified
